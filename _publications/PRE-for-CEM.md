PRE-for-CEM is an open educational resource developed to support prospective MSc Civil Engineering students at Delft University of Technology. The project provides a structured overview of prerequisite knowledge required to successfully start the master's programme and helps students identify areas where additional preparation may be beneficial. 【1-f663d5】

Developed as a Jupyter Book, the resource allows students to assess their own background knowledge and determine which subjects may require refreshing before commencing their studies. The platform improves accessibility to educational materials and supports a smoother transition into the Civil Engineering master's programme. 【1-f663d5】

The project was carried out during 2022 and 2023 as part of the educational research initiative PRE-for-CEM (PRErequisite Knowledge for Civil Engineering Master). It was funded by the TU Delft Open Education Fund and the Educational Management Team of the Faculty of Civil Engineering and Geosciences. 【1-f663d5】

This work contributed to the development of open educational resources and student support tools, demonstrating how digital learning environments can be used to improve accessibility, self-assessment and preparation for higher education in engineering. 【1-f663d5】
