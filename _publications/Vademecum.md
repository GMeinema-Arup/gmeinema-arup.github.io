The *Vademecum voor Draagconstructies van Gebouwen* is an educational resource developed for Architecture students at Delft University of Technology. It serves as a practical reference guide supporting structural design projects and engineering coursework by providing accessible structural engineering knowledge for the early stages of design. 【1-9ae8f2】

The handbook is intended exclusively for educational purposes and is not intended to be used for the final structural verification of real-world structures. It provides students with a structured overview of frequently used design information and engineering principles. 【1-9ae8f2】

The publication consists of four key components:

- **Instructions** – guidance on applying structural engineering principles in design projects.
- **Rules of Thumb** – practical engineering approximations and preliminary sizing guidance.
- **Basic Data** – reference information commonly required during conceptual and preliminary design.
- **Examples** – illustrative worked examples demonstrating structural design concepts in practice. 【1-9ae8f2】

This project contributed to the development and maintenance of educational material that helps bridge the gap between architectural design and structural engineering, providing students with a practical resource to support informed design decisions throughout their studies. 【1-9ae8f2】
