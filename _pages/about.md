<h1 class="page-title">About</h1>

<section class="about reveal">
  <p>
    I am a Graduate Bridge Engineer at <a href="https://www.arup.com/">Arup</a> based in the Amsterdam office, specialising in the analysis, assessment, strengthening, and design of bridge structures. My work focuses on developing clear, verifiable finite element and parametric models to support both the assessment of existing infrastructure and the delivery of new bridge solutions.
  </p>

  <p>
    I have contributed to a range of Dutch infrastructure projects, including concrete substructure strengthening, bridge assessments, and the design of temporary bridge structures. To support efficient and reliable engineering workflows, I regularly work with computational design and analysis tools such as Grasshopper, GSA, and SCIA Engineer.
  </p>

  <p>
    Alongside assessment and strengthening projects, I enjoy supporting conceptual bridge design studies, taking projects from early typology and feasibility investigations through to detailed structural design. My interests lie in combining traditional structural engineering with digital and parametric design approaches to create practical and innovative infrastructure solutions.
  </p>

  <p>
    My developing interests are centred on concrete bridge engineering and computational design, particularly the use of parametric modelling and automation to improve design processes and engineering decision-making.
  </p>

  <p>
    Outside of work, I enjoy running, mountaineering, photography, and reading.
  </p>

  <p>
    <a href="{{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}">Get in touch</a> if you would like to discuss bridge engineering, computational design, or potential collaboration opportunities.
  </p>
</section>
